About RavenCrew

RavenCrew is SAE MNIT's combustion engine division, mastering the art of internal combustion with precision engineering and relentless innovation. We design and build high-performance formula cars that push the limits of mechanical excellence.

With years of experience in IC engine optimization, advanced aerodynamics, and lightweight chassis design, our team has consistently delivered championship-winning machines. We believe in the perfect symphony of power, control, and driver feedback.
