About Tvaran

Tvaran Racing is SAE MNIT's electric vehicle division, leading the charge in sustainable high-performance racing. We design and build cutting-edge electric formula cars that combine speed, efficiency, and innovation.

Our team aims to compete at national and international Formula Student competitions, showcasing our expertise in electric powertrain design, battery management systems, and advanced control algorithms. We're not just building cars; we're shaping the future of motorsport.
