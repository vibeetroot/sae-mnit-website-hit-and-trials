About SAE

The Society of Automotive Engineers (SAE) at MNIT Jaipur is a dynamic community of passionate engineers, designers, and innovators dedicated to pushing the boundaries of automotive technology.

We aim to participate in prestigious competitions like XYZZ, where our teams design, build, and race high-performance formula cars. Our mission is to provide hands-on experience in automotive engineering, fostering innovation, teamwork, and technical excellence.
